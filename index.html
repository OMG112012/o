// 07 — The Minds

The Creators

Three souls. One shared obsession with the things people carry quietly. We wrote this book because we couldn't find the words anywhere else — so we made them.

Mariam
Co-Author

She has always been the one who notices things — the pause before someone answers, the way a room changes when certain people enter. She wrote this book to make space for the feelings that don't have clean names yet.

Omar
Co-Author

He grew up watching people pretend. He's been trying to write about that for years. This book is the first time it came out right — complicated, uncomfortable, and more honest than he expected it to be.

Ganah
Co-Author

She used to journal obsessively — pages and pages of things she would never say out loud. A lot of that is in here. She's terrified and relieved that it exists now, somewhere outside of her.
